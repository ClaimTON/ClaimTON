## 🐸 What is ClaimTON?

ClaimTON is a community-first airdrop project built on the **TON blockchain** the official blockchain powering Telegram's Web3 ecosystem. Our mission is simple: reward every early member with a free Telegram digital asset, just for showing up before everyone else.

We believe that in blockchain, **a strong community is worth more than any marketing budget.** So instead of ads, we invest directly in the people who believe in us first.
